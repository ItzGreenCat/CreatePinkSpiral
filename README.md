## 🍥 English Description

### **"Everything starts with a spin—from the teeth of a gear to the heart of a spiral."**

**Create: Pink Spiral** is a specialized addon for the **Create Mod** (NeoForge). More than just a collection of mechanics, it is a digital tribute to the MTF journey and the delicate beauty of transition. 

This project bridges the gap between the rigid precision of brass machinery and the fluid, transformative path of self-discovery. 

#### ✨ Project Essence
* **🧬 A Journey of Becoming**: Dedicated to the MTF experience, focusing on the themes of balance, evolution, and finding one's true rhythm.
* **🍥 The Kamaboko Symbolism**: Using the "Pink Spiral" of the fish cake as a subtle, non-explicit emblem of Pride and transformation.
* **🛠️ Evolving Mechanics**: Currently under conceptualization, focusing on integrating biochemical narratives with kinetic power.

> *"The spiral is unfolding. We are refining the path to ensure every gear rotation reflects the miracle of change."*

## 🍥 中文介绍

### **“一切皆始于旋转——从齿轮的咬合，到旋涡的核心。”**

**Create: Pink Spiral** 是一款基于 **NeoForge** 的机械动力（Create Mod）附属插件。它不仅仅是功能模块的堆砌，更是一份献给 MTF 旅程与蜕变之美的数字化致敬。

本项目旨在冰冷的黄铜机械精密感与细腻的自我追寻之路间搭建一座桥梁。

#### ✨ 项目灵魂
* **🧬 蜕变之旅**：专注于 MTF 群体的生命历程，探讨平衡、演化与寻找真实生命律动的主题。
* **🍥 鱼板符号**：以鱼板（Kamaboko）标志性的“粉红旋涡”作为含蓄的身份标识，象征着自豪与转化。
* **🛠️ 动态进化**：核心特性目前处于构思与打磨阶段，致力于将生物化学叙事与机械动力学深度融合。

> *“旋涡正在展开。我们正在精炼这条路径，以确保每一圈齿轮的转动，都能见证‘成为自我’的奇迹。”*

### 🛠️ Environment / 环境要求
- **Minecraft**: 1.20.1+
- **NeoForge**: Latest Recommended

### 🎨 Visual Identity / 视觉标识
The **Pink Spiral** (Kamaboko) represents the cycle of change. It is an "invisible" symbol of Pride, designed for those who seek their true balance in a world of brass and steam.
